# Upwork Proposal Guide

## What Works (Based on Analysis)

### The Winning Formula

1. **Personalization (Critical)**
   - Use client's name
   - Reference their specific project
   - Show you understood their problem

2. **Show Results, Not Just Skills**
   - Before/after metrics
   - Specific numbers
   - Relevant case studies

3. **Quick Response**
   - Apply within 1 hour of posting
   - First 10 proposals get 80% of interviews

4. **Direct CTA**
   - "Let's discuss your needs on a quick call"
   - Avoid: "I'm available if you're interested"

## Tips

- Customize every proposal
- Keep it under 150 words
- Add relevant portfolio link
- Follow up after 48h if no response
- Start with lower rate to get first reviews
