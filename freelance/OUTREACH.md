# Email Outreach System

## Cold Email Template

### For SaaS Customers

**Subject:** [Problem] - Quick question

Hi [Name],

I noticed [company] is [action related to problem].

We help [similar companies] [result].

Quick question: Is [pain point] still a challenge?

Best,
[Name]

---

### For Freelance Clients

**Subject:** AI development for [Company]

Hi [Name],

I build AI agents and automation systems for [industry].

Recently helped a [similar company] [result].

Would you be open to a brief call?

Best,
[Name]

---

## Sequence

1. Day 0: Initial email
2. Day 3: Follow up #1
3. Day 7: Follow up #2
4. Day 14: Final follow up

## Best Practices

- Personalize subject line
- Keep email < 100 words
- No attachments first time
- Follow up 3-4 times
- Use plain text
