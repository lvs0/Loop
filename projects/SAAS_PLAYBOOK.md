# SaaS Launch Playbook

## Week 1: Validation

### Day 1-2: Find Problem
- Browse Reddit, Hacker News, Twitter
- Look for recurring complaints
- Note: 3 problems minimum

### Day 3-4: Validate Market
- Check search volume
- Check competitor presence
- Estimate market size

### Day 5-7: Define MVP
- One核心功能
- Maximum 3 features
- Write spec document

---

## Week 2: Build

### Day 8-10: Core Development
- Set up project structure
- Build核心功能
- Simple UI (or no UI for API)

### Day 11-13: Integration
- Connect payment (LemonSqueezy/Stripe)
- Set up auth
- Add API keys system

### Day 14: Testing
- Test payment flow
- Test edge cases
- Fix bugs

---

## Week 3: Launch

### Day 15-17: Launch
- Post on Product Hunt
- Share on Twitter
- Share on Hacker News

### Day 18-19: Outreach
- Email 20 potential customers
- Post in relevant communities
- Offer free trials

### Day 20-21: Collect Feedback
- Note all feedback
- Prioritize features
- Plan v2

---

## Week 4: Monetize

### Day 22-24: Convert
- Offer paid plans
- Reach out to free users
- Set up email sequences

### Day 25-28: Optimize
- A/B test pricing
- Improve conversion
- Reduce churn

---

## Growth Framework

### Month 1-3: Product-Market Fit
- 10 paying customers
- 100% on revenue

### Month 4-6: Scale
- 50 paying customers
- Launch on marketplaces

### Month 7-12: Growth
- 100+ customers
- MRR: $5K-10K

---

## Distribution Channels

| Channel | Effort | ROI |
|---------|--------|-----|
| Product Hunt | High | Medium |
| Twitter/X | Medium | High |
| Hacker News | Medium | High |
| Reddit | Medium | Medium |
| Cold Email | High | High |
| SEO | Low | Long-term |

---

## Pricing Strategy

### Rule of Thumb
- Price at 10x customer value
- Start high, discount later
- Always have annual discount (20%)

### Example Pricing
| Tier | Price | Features |
|------|-------|----------|
| Hobby | $9/mo | 1K requests |
| Pro | $29/mo | 100K requests |
| Business | $99/mo | Unlimited |
