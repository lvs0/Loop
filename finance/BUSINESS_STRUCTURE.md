# Business Structure Guide (France)

## Options Comparison

### Auto-Entrepreneur (Micro-entreprise)
**Best for: Freelance, testing**

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Setup | Online, 15 min |
| Cost | Free (URSSAF) |
| Taxes | % of revenue |
| Ceiling | 77K EUR (services) |
| Social | ~22% of revenue |

**Taxes Rate:**
- Services: 22%
- Commerce: 12.8%
- Both: 23%

---

### SARL / EURL
**Best for: Growing business, protection**

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Setup | 1-2 weeks, ~50 EUR |
| Capital | 1 EUR minimum |
| Taxes | ~25% on profits |
| Ceiling | Unlimited |
| Social | ~80% (EURL) |

**Advantages:**
- Liability protection
- Credibility with clients
- Can hire employees

---

### SAS / SASU
**Best for: Scaling, investors**

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Setup | 2-4 weeks, ~200 EUR |
| Capital | 1 EUR minimum |
| Taxes | ~25% on profits |
| Flexibility | High |
| Social | ~70% (SASU) |

**Advantages:**
- Most flexible
- Good for SaaS
- Can issue stock

---

## Recommendation

### Phase 1: Auto-Entrepreneur
- Start freelance now
- Validate demand
- No cost, no risk

### Phase 2: SARL/EURL
- When revenue > 30K EUR/year
- Need liability protection
- Want to hire

### Phase 3: SAS
- When scaling to 100K+
- Need investor
- More flexibility

---

## Action Steps

1. Register on urssaf.fr (auto-entrepreneur)
2. Choose activity: services informatiques
3. Get tax number
4. Open business bank account (optional)
5. Start invoicing
