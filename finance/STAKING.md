## Action Plan

### Step 1: Choose Platform
- Start with Coinbase or Kraken (easiest)
- Later: Move to Lido for better yields

### Step 2: Buy Tokens
- Buy ETH or SOL on exchange
- Minimum: 500 EUR recommended

### Step 3: Stake
- Enable staking on platform
- Rewards auto-compound

## Risk Management
- Max 10% of portfolio in crypto
- Keep private keys secure
- Use hardware wallet for large amounts
- Diversify across 2-3 platforms
